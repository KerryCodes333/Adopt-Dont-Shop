About Us!

Our founders, accountant Jane and veterinarian Philip Simpson were very much cat people. Since they had been fostering cats throughout their careers, it seemed only natural at their retirement to move to Rosebush Farm, York, and to build our stunning premees. Now managed by Jane and Phillip's niece, Claire Woods, the attitude that 'there's a saucer of milk for whoever wants it' has remained but we now work with the RSPCA, Cats Protection, and WWF to ensure the safety of cats across the UK.

We are based in York. You can find us on Google Maps here.

We rely on funding to keep our organisation operational. We are currently funded by the RSPCA, Cats Protection, WWF and the National Lottery Fund. If you would like to support us, please visit out donation page.

We are a Royal Canin affiliate. Please visit their website here.
