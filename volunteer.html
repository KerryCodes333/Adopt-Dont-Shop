Volunteer with us

Have a look at the roles available below to see how you could help!

Cat Care Assistant

Do you love cats? Would you like some valuable animal care work experience? Help us look after our cats in their temporary homes at our shelter! We need cat loving volunteers to feed, play with and clean up after all our fabulous felines!

Receptionist

Do you love cats? Do you have a few hours a week to spare? Join us as a volunteer receptionist! This role involves welcoming visitors to our shelter and helping us process adoption enquiries.

Charity Shop Cashier

Do you love cats? Do you have an eye for second-hand fashion? Help us run our shop! We need you to serve customers, sort through donations and help make our store look amazing!
